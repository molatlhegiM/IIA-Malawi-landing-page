What’s in Store for You?

This masterclass is designed for:

What Attendees Will Learn in a 3-Day Power BI Training

• Fundamentals of Power BI and data visualization.
• How to create professional, interactive dashboards.
• Techniques for transforming raw data into actionable insights.
• Real-world applications and case studies.
• The role of data in driving business and professional success.

Day 1: Foundations of Power BI

  • Understanding Power BI: Overview of the tool, its purpose, and how it integrates with other Microsoft products.
  • Data Import and Transformation:
    • Connecting to different data sources (Excel, databases, online services, etc.).
    • Cleaning and transforming data using Power Query.
  • Data Modeling:
    • Creating relationships between tables.
    • Designing an efficient data model.
    • Introduction to DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for basic calculations.
  • Visualization Basics:
    • Creating simple reports with charts, tables, and slicers.
    • Understanding visualization best practices.

Why It's Beneficial: Participants will understand the fundamentals of data preparation and visualization, enabling them to start creating basic, insightful reports for decision-making.

Day 2: Advanced Reporting and Analysis

  • Advanced DAX:
    • Using advanced functions like time intelligence, CALCULATE, and FILTER.
    • Writing measures for complex calculations.
  • Interactive Dashboards:
    • Designing dashboards with drill-throughs and tooltips.
    • Adding interactivity with slicers and bookmarks.
  • Custom Visuals: Incorporating and customizing third-party visuals from the Power BI marketplace.
  • Data Storytelling:
    • Structuring reports to tell a compelling story.
    • Best practices for user experience and navigation.
